Common Questions

How do I delete my data?

Open the app → Settings → Data & Privacy → “Clear Saved Game” or “Clear Game History”. All data is stored locally and can be deleted from within the app.

I purchased Premium but it’s not unlocked

Open the app → Settings → “Restore Purchases”. Ensure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

Why am I seeing ads?

Ads appear in the free version to support development. Premium removes ads entirely as a one‑time purchase.

Is there a French website?

The app is localized in English and French. This website is in English. If you need assistance in French, feel free to email us — we’ll gladly help.

Bug Reports & Feedback

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version

  • Screenshots or screen recordings if possible
