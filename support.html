FAQs

How do I start a new game?

Open BouleBoard, select New Game, choose your mode (solo, teams, or free-for-all), add players or teams, and tap Start.

How are points calculated?

Points equal the number of boules your team has closer to the cochonnet than the opponent’s nearest boule. Singles max 3 points per round; doubles/triples max 6.

Why does the app ask for camera or photo access?

Only to capture or select player avatar photos. You can use BouleBoard without granting these permissions.

What’s included in Premium?

Unlimited players/games, advanced stats, avatar customization, and an ad-free experience. $10 lifetime or $1.99/month.

Ads in BouleBoard

Free tier may show ads via Google AdMob. We aim to keep ad timing unobtrusive. Premium disables ads.
